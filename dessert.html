Chocolate Strawberry Brownies

Love at first bite.

YIELDS PREP TIME TOTAL TIME
16 SERVINGS 15 MINS 1 HOUR

Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix, plus ingredients called for on box
  • 1 1/2 c. halved strawberries
  • 2 1/3 c. semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 c. heavy cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° and line an 8”-x-8” pan with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare brownie batter according to package instructions.
  3. Make ganache
  4. Pour the hot cream over chocolate chips. Let sit for 5 minutes, then whisk constantly until the sauce is smooth.
  5. Refrigerate brownies until the ganache has set, about 15 minutes. Slice into squares and serve cold.
